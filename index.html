AI/ML Systems Security Lead

I'm a security engineer and leader with over 15 years of experience in low-level systems security, software development, and vulnerability research. I thrive on tackling complex security problems at scale.

For the past decade at Google, I've focused on making our core products and infrastructure fundamentally more secure. This journey has involved building widely-used security tools like Honggfuzz and NSJail, and leading a world-class team dedicated to software isolation and fuzzing.

My work has been recognized with two Pwnie Award nominations (2016 and 2017) for critical vulnerability discoveries.

Current Focus: Securing the AI Frontier

Since 2023, I lead the AI/ML Systems Security team at Google. Our mission is to protect the company's foundational models and the infrastructure they run on. We're exploring new frontiers in confidential computing, hardening machine learning frameworks against novel attacks, and building the foundational security technologies to ensure the safe development and deployment of AI.
